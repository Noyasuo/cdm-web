Key Supplier Policies

By becoming a supplier, you are agreeing to follow the guidelines set forth in our policies. These are designed to ensure a smooth, efficient, and professional relationship. Below are the essential points:

1. Timely Delivery of Products

Suppliers must ensure the timely delivery of products as per agreed schedules. Delayed deliveries can cause disruptions and penalties may be imposed.

2. Adherence to Quality Standards

All products provided by suppliers must meet the quality standards specified in the agreement. Regular quality checks will be performed to maintain consistency.

3. Clear Communication Regarding Stock Levels and Pricing

Suppliers must provide transparent information about stock availability, pricing, and any potential changes to their product offerings. Regular communication is key to avoiding misunderstandings.

4. Packaging and Labeling Requirements

Proper packaging and accurate labeling of products are critical for the safe and efficient delivery of goods. All products must be packaged to prevent damage during transit and labeled according to our specifications.

5. Returns and Refunds

In case of defective or damaged products, suppliers must adhere to our return and refund policies. A clear process for returns must be followed to ensure customer satisfaction.

6. Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure

Suppliers must maintain confidentiality regarding any proprietary or sensitive information shared during the partnership. Any breach of confidentiality can result in termination of the supplier relationship.

7. Compliance with Laws

Suppliers must comply with all relevant local, state, and international laws, including labor laws, environmental regulations, and industry-specific rules.

8. Product Categories

Seller must upload a product that are related into Cleaning supply, Office supply, Electronics and parts. We already provided a sample product on the Catalogue section, uploading product not related to the given categories are probihited

9. Customer Service and Support

Suppliers are expected to provide excellent customer service, addressing any issues or inquiries promptly. We strive for a high level of satisfaction in every transaction.
