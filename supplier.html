How to Register as a Supplier

To start providing your products or services to our platform, please follow the steps below:

  1. Visit the registration page.
  2. Complete the necessary information, including your business details and product catalog.
  3. Submit your registration for verification.
  4. Wait for Admin aprroval.
  5. After approved you can use your credentials now to login into our provided desktop application.
